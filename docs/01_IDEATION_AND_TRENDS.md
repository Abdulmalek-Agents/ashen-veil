## 2. Primary evidence

- Black Myth: Wukong proved that mythologically-themed souls-likes can hit ~$1B in revenue.
- Lies of P sold 1M+ in its first month, showing indie souls-likes win with style and tight combat.
- FromSoftware's Miyazaki has credited gamer demand for punishing difficulty as the source of the genre's birth and ongoing popularity.
- Steam tag analysis: 'soulslike' tag continues to attract above-average revenue per release.

## 3. Reddit / Discord signal

- r/Soulslikes (~500k members): top complaints are 100-hour bloat, opaque storytelling. We respond with 6 focused missions and a Claude lore companion.
- r/IndieDev: 'soulslike with X twist' (anime, magic, etc.) consistently pitched.
- Souls-like Discord communities reward devs who post combat GIFs early.
